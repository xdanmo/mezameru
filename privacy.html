PRIVACY POLICY

EFFECTIVE DATE: JULY 2, 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION & STORAGE

MEZAMERU is a client-side application. We do not operate a central database or server. All of your habit data, settings, and profile information are stored exclusively in two places:

A) Locally on your device (using your browser's Local Storage).
B) Securely within your own personal Dropbox account.

2. DROPBOX INTEGRATION

MEZAMERU requests access to your Dropbox account solely for the purpose of reading and writing a single backup file (mezameru_data.json). The developer of this app has absolutely zero access to your Dropbox files, your authentication tokens, or your habit data.

3. DATA SHARING & ANALYTICS

Because we do not collect your data on our servers, we do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties. There are no hidden tracking scripts or external analytics engines monitoring your habits.

4. YOUR CONSENT

By using MEZAMERU, you consent to the processing of your data strictly as described in this policy. You can revoke the app's access to your Dropbox account at any time via your Dropbox Security settings.
